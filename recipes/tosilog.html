Tosilog

Description

This is a killer filipino breakfast recipe that is easy to make. The recipe consists of Tocino (cured pork), sunny side up egg, and garlic fried rice.

Serving size: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 package of Tocino (can be found in asian supermarket)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1.5 cups of cooked, chilled white rice
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • canola oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Lay tinfoil on a baking sheet.
  2. Once preheated, bake the tocino for ~20 min or until fat around it is black and a char on the meat develops.
  3. In a medium frying pan or skillet, add enough canola oil to create a very thin layer that covers the entire cooking surface area and let it heat up for 4 min.
  4. While pan/skillet with oil is heating up, finely chop the garlic.
  5. Add chopped garlic and stir occasionally until golden brown.
  6. Add the cooked, chilled rice. (best if refigerated overnight but 20 min in the freezer after freshly cooked is good enough.)
  7. Break up and mix the rice until you can see that most of the rice is covered in oil. Add more oil if it looks too dry.
  8. Empty rice from pan/skillet onto a plate and cover to retain heat.
  9. Add about 2 tbsp of more oil. Crack 4 eggs and and cover pan/skillet with a lid until desired done-ness.
  10. Plate breakfast by dividing the garlic fried rice, eggs, and tocino.
