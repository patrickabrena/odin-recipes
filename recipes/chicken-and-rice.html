Chicken and Rice

Description

This is my favourite dinner dish because it's flavourful, simple and it can be made in under 30 min with prep time included!

Serving size: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless and boneless chicken thighs
  • 1.5 cups of washed white rice
  • 1/2 medium onion (any kind)
  • 2 tbsp of salt
  • 2 tbsp of pepper
  • 2 tbsp of smoked paprika
  • 2 tbsp of onion powder
  • 2 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 4 cups of finely chopped spinach
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped green onion
  • 1 decent sized dollop of butter
  • 4 tbsp of canola oil
  • 3 cups of chicken stock

Instructions

  1. In a medium frying pan/skillet add the canola oil and let it heat up for 4 min on medium heat
  2. Mix all the spices together in a bowl. Coat chicken thighs in the spice mix.
  3. Sear the chicken on both sides until it browns. (~4 min on each side. Doesn't have to be fully cooked.)
  4. Remove chicken thighs and set aside. In the same pan add the butter.
  5. Add the onions and stir until softened.
  6. Add the washed rice and continuously stir for a couple min.
  7. Add the chicken back in and cover lid. Let it the rice and chicken cook for 10-12 min.
  8. While that's cooking, chop up spinach and green onions.
  9. After the 10-12 min, turn off heat and mix in the veggies. Serve and plate when still hot.
